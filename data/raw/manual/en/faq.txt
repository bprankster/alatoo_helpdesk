FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS — ADMISSIONS TO ALA-TOO INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITY (AIU)

=== ORT AND ADMISSIONS ===

Q: What is the minimum ORT score to apply to AIU?
A: 110 points — the same minimum for all faculties. Set by the Ministry of Education of the Kyrgyz Republic.

Q: Are there state-funded (budget) places at AIU?
A: No. AIU is a private university. There are no budget places. Discounts replace them.

Q: How is the discount calculated?
A: The discount depends on your ORT score. For example: 191–195 points = 25% discount, 196–200 = 30%.
   Gold certificate = 100% discount (free tuition).

Q: Can I use my ORT score from a previous year?
A: No. Only the current year's ORT score is accepted.

Q: When does the discount stop?
A: The discount is cancelled if a student gets 4 academic debts in one semester.

Q: Can I combine multiple discounts?
A: No. A student may use only one discount — the highest available.

=== DOCUMENTS AND PAYMENT ===

Q: What documents do I need to apply?
A: Original school leaving certificate, current ORT certificate, passport, medical certificate (form 086/u),
   6 passport photos (3×4 cm), application form.

Q: How does payment work?
A: Initial payment is 30% of the annual tuition. You can then pay 50% at the start of each semester.
   Payment in Kyrgyz som (KGS) only.

Q: Where do I submit documents?
A: Via the Ministry of Education portal: https://2020.edu.gov.kg/vuz
   Or in person: 1/10 Ankara St., Tunguch district, Bishkek, Block D, 1st floor.

=== ENGLISH LANGUAGE ===

Q: Do I need to know English?
A: Yes. Level B1 Intermediate is required. Confirmed by the free internal AIU EPT test.

Q: What language are classes taught in?
A: All programs at AIU are taught in English.

=== ADDITIONAL ORT SUBJECTS ===

Q: What additional subjects are needed for the IT faculty?
A: Faculty of Engineering and IT requires: Mathematics ≥60 AND Physics ≥60 (mandatory).

Q: What additional subjects are needed for medicine?
A: Medical Faculty (General Medicine): Biology ≥60 AND Chemistry ≥60 (mandatory).

Q: Do other faculties require additional subjects?
A: No. The Faculty of Humanities, Faculty of Economics, and Faculty of Social Sciences
   do not require additional ORT subjects.

=== ALTERNATIVES ===

Q: My ORT score is below 110. Can I still study at AIU?
A: Yes — apply to AIU IT&Business College (no ORT required). Duration: 1 year 10 months.
   After graduation you can enter directly into the 2nd year of the university.
   Red diploma graduates receive a 20% discount on AIU tuition.
