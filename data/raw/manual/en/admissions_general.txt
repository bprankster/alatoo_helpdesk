ADMISSIONS TO ALA-TOO INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITY (AIU) — GENERAL INFORMATION
Academic Year 2024-2025

=== MINIMUM ORT SCORE ===
Minimum ORT score to apply to AIU: 110 points (set by the Ministry of Education of the Kyrgyz Republic).
This requirement is the same for ALL faculties.
AIU is a private university — there are NO state-funded (budget) places. Discounts replace budget seats.
ORT scores from previous years are NOT accepted — current year only.

=== ADDITIONAL ORT SUBJECT REQUIREMENTS ===
Faculty of Engineering and IT: Mathematics ≥60 AND Physics ≥60 (mandatory).
Medical Faculty (General Medicine): Biology ≥60 AND Chemistry ≥60 (mandatory).
All other faculties: no additional subject requirements.

=== ENGLISH LANGUAGE REQUIREMENT ===
Level B1 Intermediate. Confirmed by the internal AIU EPT test (free of charge).
All programs at AIU are taught in English.

=== HOW TO APPLY ===
Submit documents via the Ministry of Education portal: https://2020.edu.gov.kg/vuz
Initial payment: 30% of the annual tuition fee.
Option to pay 50% at the start of each semester.
Payment accepted only in Kyrgyz som (KGS) at the NBKR exchange rate.

=== REQUIRED DOCUMENTS ===
- Original secondary school certificate (attestat)
- Current year ORT certificate
- Passport or birth certificate
- Medical certificate (form 086/u)
- 6 passport photos (3×4 cm)
- Application form

=== AIU COLLEGE (alternative if ORT score is below 110) ===
AIU IT&Business College — admission without ORT.
Duration: 1 year 10 months.
After graduation — direct entry to the 2nd year of the university.
Red diploma graduates receive a 20% discount on AIU tuition.
