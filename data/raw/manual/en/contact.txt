=== ABOUT ALA-TOO (AIU) ===
Ala-Too International University (AIU) is a private university in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan.
Teaching language: English.
Faculties: Engineering and IT, Humanities, Economics and Management,
Medical Faculty, Faculty of Social Sciences.
All programs: Bachelor's degree (4 years), except General Medicine (6 years).
