TUITION DISCOUNTS AT AIU BASED ON ORT RESULTS — 2024-2025

AIU is a private university. There are no state-funded (budget) places.
Discounts replace budget seats.
A student may use ONLY ONE discount (the highest available).

=== ORT-BASED DISCOUNTS ===
Gold certificate:   100% discount (free tuition)
Score 216–220:       50% discount
Score 211–215:       45% discount
Score 206–210:       40% discount
Score 201–205:       35% discount
Score 196–200:       30% discount
Score 191–195:       25% discount
Score 186–190:       20% discount
Score 181–185:       15% discount
Score 176–180:       10% discount
Score 171–175:        5% discount
Score 110–170:        0% (admitted, no discount)

=== OTHER DISCOUNTS ===
AIU College graduates:                               10%
AIU IT&Business College red diploma graduates:       20%
MOE Sapat lycée graduates:                           15%
Orphans (both parents deceased):                     40%
Loss of one parent:                                  10%
Families with 4+ children under 22:                 10%

=== IMPORTANT CONDITIONS ===
- Discount is cancelled if a student accumulates 4 academic debts in a semester.
- Only one discount can be used (the highest one).
- Payment accepted only in Kyrgyz som (KGS) at the NBKR rate.
- Initial payment: 30% of the annual tuition.
- Option to pay 50% at the start of each semester.
- Previous years' ORT scores are NOT accepted.
